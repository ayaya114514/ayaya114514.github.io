### 1. [Robustness and reliability of single-cell regulatory multi-omics with deep mitochondrial mutation profiling](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.08.23.609473v2)

- **算力需求**：未提及
- **复现成本**：困难，需要深度 mtDNA profiling、单细胞多组学实验和 ground-truth lineage validation

### 2. [Integrating morphology and gene expression of neural cells in unpaired single-cell data using GeoAdvAE](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.11.19.689368v3)

**作者**：Du, J. T., Chartrand, T., Jayadev, S. 等  
**发表日期**：2026-06-30  
**分类**：genomics  
**Paper ID**：`biorxiv:10.1101/2025.11.19.689368`

#### 核心贡献
提出 GeoAdvAE，在形态学和 scRNA-seq 不成对测量的情况下，把 neural cell morphology 与 gene expression 嵌入共享 latent space。该方法在 patch-seq ground truth 上优于多类 diagonal integration baseline，并在 Alzheimer model microglia 数据中发现形态-转录组轴和疾病相关信号。

- **模型/算法**：geometry-aware adversarial autoencoder、modality-specific VAE、Gromov-Wasserstein regularizer、adversarial discriminator、integrated gradients
- **关键库与工具**：GeoAdvAE
- **数据集**：patch-seq neurons、98 CAJAL-quantified microglial morphologies、31,948 5xFAD scRNA-seq transcriptomes
- **开源情况**：代码公开：https://github.com/turbodu222/GeoAdVAE

#### 上手难度
- **前置知识**：single-cell transcriptomics、cell morphology quantification、VAE、optimal transport、adversarial learning
- **算力需求**：未提及
- **复现成本**：中等，代码开源但需要跨模态单细胞数据和模型训练经验
- **适合谁读**：硕博生 / 研究员 / 工业界工程师
